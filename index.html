Comfort with Crunchit

When I first came to Canada, the first thing I started to miss, was food from home.

Crunchit gave me comfort because the closes thing I could find to home, was the food and snacks that I couldn't find here.

What is Crunchit?

Crunchit is a market place that sells snacks. If you're missing something from home, give Crunchit a try.

Crunchit was also developed as a way for you to save money by not having to pay insane markups because the foods you see everyday back home, are considered exotic here.
